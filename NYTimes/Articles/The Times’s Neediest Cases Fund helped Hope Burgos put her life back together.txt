Long before she went into labor, it was obvious to Hope Burgos what she would name her first daughter: Serenity, a quality that had long been absent from Ms. Burgos’s life, but one that she had always hoped to create for her children. The name came from a prayer that she said as a teenager living in a group home and struggling with her mental health, “God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change, courage to change the things I can, and wisdom to know the difference.”
