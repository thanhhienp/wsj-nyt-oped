If you were to pick a year in the past century or so and ask me to name something that happened then, my answer would almost certainly have to do with sports. Nineteen-seventy? Willis Reed hobbles back to help the New York Knicks win the N.B.A. Finals. Nineteen-twenty-seven? The Babe Ruth-Lou Gehrig New York Yankees, the greatest team of all time. Nineteen-eighty? “Do you believe in miracles?!”
