At some American hospitals, ventilators are already in short supply, as a result of the coronavirus pandemic. But what happens when there aren’t enough to go around? How do we decide who gets a chance to live and who dies? The video above argues that the United States needs a national rationing plan. Individual doctors and hospitals can’t be left to make these agonizing choices on their own, and the question of who lives and who dies should not depend on what state a patient lives in.
